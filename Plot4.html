Prevailing Wind Four Factors Read More!

Wind is driven by changes in atmospheric pressure resulting from uneven heating. Wind speed is influenced by factors that relate to atmospheric pressure such as how quickly the pressure changes. As our graph shows, wind speed itself is not closely correlated with latitude. However, the direction and force of wind speed are surprisingly predictable and have been leveraged for centuries in all sorts of fields. Click the links above to learn more!
